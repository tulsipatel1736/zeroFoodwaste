Our Mission

Our mission is to empower primary school students to reduce food waste and protect the planet through our interactive and engaging interclass food waste challenge.

We aim to inspire a lifelong commitment to sustainability by fostering environmental awareness and promoting responsible food consumption habits among the next generation.
